Result-oriented team

ZIKZAK Architects design studio has been on the market for over 7 years and during this time the best team of specialists has been formed, which will always emphasize the individuality of each customer, using the latest trends and acquired experience. One of the company's advantages is a "deep immersion" into the project through the history of the customer in order to form a holistic concept designed to solve the task in the most productive way.

ZIKZAK Architects is a dynamic and committed group of architects and interior designers who love design and the creative process. Our experience and constant research of trends allows us to meet expectations within the budget and translate a bold idea into non-standard design solutions. To get a truly functional and unsurpassed design of an office, apartment, restaurant, shopping and entertainment complex or a hotel, the right decision would be to contact the design studio ZIKZAK Architects for help!
